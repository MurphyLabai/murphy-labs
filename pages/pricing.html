Common Questions

What's the difference between Diamond and Platinum?

Both tiers unlock the ability to upload your own custom content — music, images, and themes. Platinum goes further by giving you access to our biggest, most feature-rich apps that require more to build and host. Think of Platinum as the "everything" tier.

Do I need an account to use the free apps?

No. All free tools work instantly with no sign-up required. Accounts are only needed when you upgrade to Diamond or Platinum to unlock custom uploads.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep Diamond or Platinum access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the device limit work?

Your Diamond or Platinum subscription works on up to 2 devices. This covers most individual users and power users who work across a phone and laptop.

What happens to my uploads if I cancel?

Your custom uploads stay saved in your account. When you resubscribe, everything is there exactly as you left it.
